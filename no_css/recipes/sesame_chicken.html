Sesame chicken recipe

Description

A good sesame chicken recipe for six. Total time is around one hour.

    Ingredients

  • flour
  • cornstarch
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • soy sauce
  • water
  • sesame oil
  • sesame sauce

    Directions

  1. Prepare the battered chicken
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Heat olive oil in a deep fryer
  4. Carefully drop in battered chicken pieces
  5. Place fried chicken pieces onto a serving platter, and pour the hot sauce overtop. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds to garnish.
